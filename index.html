About Me

Hi! I'm Jerry Marc T. Hermoso, 20 years old fresh college graduate from Angelicum College took up BS in Computer Science and I'm an amateur front end web developer.

I'm focused more on the client-side of the web and has very few ideas on how the server-side works. I have a basic to intermediate knowledge in HTML5, CSS3, JS, JQuery, Bootstrap, Github, AJAX, Responsive Web Design, and UX/UI Design.

I love creating, designing, giving effects to web pages from scratch, or with templates, or with psd designs.

My goal is to become a full stack web developer and be known in creating awesome and fancy websites.
